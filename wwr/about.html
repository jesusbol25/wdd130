History

Canaima Rafting was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to share the magic of rivers with the world. Our journey began in 2024 when a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, inspired by their love of rafting and the natural beauty of Canaima-Venezuela, decided to turn their dreams into a reality.

Armed with a single raft and an unshakable belief in the power of adventure, they set out to create a company that offered more than just rafting trips—it offered memories that would last a lifetime. In those early days, we operated out of a humble riverside cabin, welcoming our first guests as friends and introducing them to the wonders of the river.
